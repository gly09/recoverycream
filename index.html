Simple Application

How to Use the Recovery Cream

01
Cleanse the Area
Start with clean, dry skin. Gently pat the target area dry before applying.
02
Apply a Small Amount
Dispense a pea-sized amount. A little goes a long way with this concentrated botanical formula.
03
Massage Into Skin
Massage gently in a circular motion until fully absorbed. The non-greasy formula absorbs quickly.
04
Use Consistently
For best results, apply 2–3 times daily or as directed by your healthcare provider.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the cream contain THC? +
No. The Recovery Cream uses broad-spectrum CBD distillate that has been independently verified to contain 0.0% THC. This is confirmed by third-party laboratory testing using HPLC-DAD methodology at an ISO/IEC 17025 accredited lab (SC Laboratories, Inc.). The Certificate of Analysis is available upon request by contacting our team with your batch number.
How often can I use it? +
Use as needed — morning, night, or during flare-ups. Most apply 2–3 times daily. A little goes a long way.
Is it safe for pregnancy or breastfeeding? +
No THC, no harmful additives — safe for topical use during pregnancy. Check with your doctor if unsure.
What does "broad-spectrum" mean? +
Broad-spectrum CBD contains CBD along with other naturally occurring cannabinoids found in hemp — such as CBG, CBN, CBC, and CBDV — but with THC removed. This differs from CBD isolate (CBD only) and full-spectrum (which retains trace THC). Our COA confirms the cannabinoid profile of every batch, with 0.0% Delta 9-THC and 0.0% Delta 8-THC detected.
What makes this better than other creams? +
It's physician-formulated, 1500mg CBD, and COA-verified — no fluff, no fillers, no THC.
Can I use this alongside other medications? +
It's topical and has no known drug interactions. Still, check with your provider for personalized advice.
How fast does it work? +
The formula is fast-absorbing and non-greasy. Results vary by individual.
Will this show up on a drug test? +
No. It's THC-free and third-party tested to verify 0.0% THC. Safe for drug-tested professionals.
What areas of the body can I use it on? +
Anywhere you experience pain or inflammation — joints, neck, back, knees, feet, hands. Avoid eyes or broken skin.
How big is the jar? How long does it last? +
Each jar contains 2 oz (57g). For most users, that lasts 2–4 weeks depending on frequency and area of use.
Is this made in the USA? +
Yes — proudly formulated and manufactured in the USA.
